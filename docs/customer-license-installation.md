# KiraTakipPro — License Installation Guide

_For customers and end users._

---

## What is a license file?

When you purchase or subscribe to KiraTakipPro, support will provide you with a license file.
This file has the extension `.ktplicense` (for example: `CUSTOMER-001-a3f7b2c1.ktplicense`).

The license file:

- **Activates KiraTakipPro on your registered computer.** Without it, the app will show an
  activation screen instead of opening normally.
- **Is tied to one specific computer.** If you change computers, contact support for a
  replacement license for your new machine.
- **Is not your app data.** It does not contain your rental records, tenant information, or
  payment history. Keep your app data backups separately.
- **Can be reissued.** If the file is lost or damaged, contact support — they can send a
  replacement.

Keep the original email or download link from support until the license is successfully
installed. You will not need the file again once installed, but having it as a backup is helpful.

---

## Before you begin — information support may need

If you contact support about licensing, you may be asked for some or all of the following.
These items are safe to share:

| What | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| Your **customer name / customer ID** | Provided by support when your license was issued |
| **App version** | Shown in the About screen or window title bar |
| **Machine fingerprint** | Shown on the activation screen inside KiraTakipPro (see Section 2) |
| **License ID** | Shown on the activation/license status screen, or visible in the `.ktplicense` file if you open it in a text editor |
| **Screenshot of the error message** | Use Windows Snipping Tool or `Win + Shift + S` |

### What you must never send to support

- Your KiraTakipPro PIN or management account password.
- Your rental/tenant/payment data backup file — unless support specifically requests it for
  a data recovery issue.
- Any file from your Windows registry or internal system folders.
- Any file not specifically requested by support.

Support will never ask for your PIN or password. If anyone claiming to be support asks for
these, do not provide them.

---

## 1. Getting your machine fingerprint

KiraTakipPro uses a unique identifier for your computer. When you first open the app without
a license, the activation screen will display your **machine fingerprint** — a long string of
letters and numbers.

**To get your machine fingerprint:**

1. Open KiraTakipPro. If no license is installed, the activation screen opens automatically.
2. Find the field labelled **"Machine fingerprint"** on the activation screen.
3. Click the **Copy** button next to it.
4. Paste it into your email or message to support.

> Your machine fingerprint is safe to send. It is a one-way code — it cannot be used to
> access your computer or your data.

**Do not open the Windows Registry** or try to find the fingerprint manually. The app shows it
for you directly.

---

## 2. Receiving your license file from support

Support will send your `.ktplicense` file by email or a secure download link.

When you receive it:

1. **Save the file** to a location you can find easily, such as your **Downloads** folder or
   your Desktop.
2. **Do not rename the file.** Keep the filename exactly as received
   (e.g. `CUSTOMER-001-a3f7b2c1.ktplicense`).
3. **Do not open or edit the file** in a text editor. Opening it to read it is harmless, but
   editing even a single character will invalidate it.
4. Keep the original email from support until the license is confirmed working.

---

## 3. Installing (importing) your license

Once you have the `.ktplicense` file saved on your computer:

1. **Open KiraTakipPro.** The activation screen will appear if no license is installed.
2. Click **"Import license"** on the activation screen.
3. A file browser will open. Navigate to where you saved the `.ktplicense` file and select it.
4. KiraTakipPro will verify the license automatically.
5. If the license is valid and matches your computer, the app will open normally.
6. **Restart the app** if prompted.

After a successful import, your license is stored inside the app. You do not need to keep the
original `.ktplicense` file in a specific location — but keeping a copy (in your email or a
safe folder) is recommended in case you ever need to reinstall.

---

## 4. What to do if activation fails

### "No license file found" or activation screen opens every time

Your license has not been imported yet, or was not imported correctly.

- Follow the steps in Section 3 to import your `.ktplicense` file.
- If you have already imported but the screen reappears, try importing again.
- If you do not have a license file yet, contact support.

---

### "License file is damaged" or "invalid license"

The file may have been corrupted during download, or edited accidentally.

- Download or request a fresh copy of the license file from support.
- Make sure you save the file without opening or editing it.
- Try importing the new copy.

---

### "This license is registered to a different computer"

Your license is tied to the machine fingerprint of the computer it was issued for. This error
means the fingerprint on file does not match your current computer.

Common causes:
- You installed the app on a different computer.
- Windows was reinstalled on your computer (this can change your computer's identifier).
- You moved to new hardware.

**What to do:**
1. Open KiraTakipPro and note your current machine fingerprint from the activation screen.
2. Contact support with your customer name/ID and the new machine fingerprint.
3. Support will issue a replacement license for your current computer.

---

### "License expired"

Your subscription or time-limited license period has ended.

- Contact support to renew. Provide your customer ID.
- Support will issue a new license with an updated expiry date.
- Import the new license file following the steps in Section 3.

> KiraTakipPro will display a warning banner approximately 14 days before your license
> expires. This gives you time to contact support and renew before the expiry date.

---

### "Machine identity could not be determined"

In rare cases, KiraTakipPro may be unable to read your computer's unique identifier. This is
not a problem with your license file — it is usually caused by a restricted or unusual
system configuration.

- Try restarting your computer and opening KiraTakipPro again.
- If the message persists, contact support and include a screenshot of the error.

---

## 5. Changing computers

If you replace your computer or move to a new machine:

1. **Your existing license will not work on the new computer.** The license is registered to
   the machine fingerprint of your old computer.
2. Open KiraTakipPro on the **new computer**. The activation screen will show your new machine
   fingerprint.
3. Copy the new machine fingerprint and send it to support along with your customer name/ID.
4. Support will issue a replacement license for the new computer.
5. Import the replacement license following Section 3.

> Your rental data, tenant records, and backups are separate from the license. Use the app's
> built-in backup and export tools to transfer your data to the new computer.

---

## 6. If your license file is lost

If you no longer have the `.ktplicense` file and cannot import it:

1. Contact support.
2. Provide your **customer name / customer ID** and your current **machine fingerprint** (from
   the activation screen).
3. Support can resend the original license file or issue a replacement.

You do not need to provide anything else. Licenses can always be reissued.

---

## 7. Offline use

KiraTakipPro verifies your license locally on your computer. **No internet connection is
required** to use the app once your license is installed.

- The license file does not call home or contact any server.
- If you are on an air-gapped or offline network, you can receive your license file via email
  on another device and transfer it to your computer using a USB drive.
- Import the license file following Section 3, even if your computer has no internet access.

---

## 8. Your license is not your data

It is important to understand the difference:

| | License file (`.ktplicense`) | App data backup (`.ktpbackup`) |
|---|---|---|
| **What it is** | Activates the app on your computer | A copy of your rental, tenant, and payment records |
| **Tied to one computer?** | Yes | No — can be restored on any computer |
| **Lost/damaged?** | Contact support — always reissuable | Use the app's built-in backup restore |
| **Included in app backups?** | No | Yes |

Use KiraTakipPro's built-in **Backup** feature regularly to protect your rental records.
The license file is managed separately and does not need to be backed up by you — support
can always reissue it.
